01. about me

Who I Am

Hey! I'm Muhatarima Medha — a Full Stack Developer and a math lover from Dhaka, Bangladesh.

My journey into coding actually started with mathematics. I've always been obsessed with solving puzzles and playing around with numbers just for fun. At some point, I realized I could use Python to solve those problems faster and smarter — and honestly, that changed everything for me. One small script turned into curiosity, and curiosity turned into a passion for building things.

Now I spend most of my time creating full-stack web apps with React, Next.js, Node.js, Express, and MongoDB. There's something super satisfying about turning an idea into a real, working product people can interact with.

When I'm not coding, I'm probably dreaming about my next mountain trip, walking through nature, or randomly doing mental math in my head for no reason at all.

I also love teaching. I've guided students in both Math Olympiad and academic mathematics, and I enjoy making complex ideas feel simple and approachable. For me, teaching is just another form of problem-solving — helping someone see things from a new perspective until everything finally clicks.
